Tournament Rules - Final Version

1. Team & Player Rules
- 4 teams total
- 7 players per team
- 2 players per team are flexible players (decided by captain)
- Flexible players can only substitute within their own team (not opponents)
- Impact player can bat, bowl, and field (not required to be a flexible player)

2. Availability and Substitutions
- When someone in the team is injured or unavailable, captain can ask any of the other flexible players to play in their team
- Flexible players from the opposition are not allowed to play
- Team players can replace flexible players at any point of time in the match
- If someone is not available to bat, they will be considered retired out
- No batsman is allowed to bat twice in an innings

3. Match Setup
- 5 fielders + 1 keeper + 1 bowler will be on field
- Batting team will have 7 wickets
- Minimum 5 bowlers mandatory
- Maximum limit for a bowler is 2 overs

4. Tournament Rules
- Average of win margin (4s) will be used as net run rate
- Top 2 teams will play finals
- Formal scoring will be done through Stumps app

5. Umpire Rules
- Umpire can be chosen by both captains
- Every match will have 2 umpires (1 straight and 1 off/leg)
- Umpires will have the final call
- Same umpires for both teams. No side changes

6. Boundary and Ground Rules
- Cones placed by umpire define the boundary
- Obstruction by tree or umpire will not count as a four
- Ball touching boundary line is considered a boundary

7. Batting Rules
- No batting change mid-over unless injured
- Batsman can retire after over (1 per team)
- Injured batsman can return anytime

8. Bowling Rules
- No bowling change mid-over unless injured
- Only 1 foot warning per bowler per match
- Beamers and multiple bounce balls are No balls

9. Conduct Rules
- No shouting or distraction allowed
- Violations lead to demerit points
- Each demerit point removes 1 boundary from score

10. Other Rules
- All other rules remain as currently played
